## [1.0.0] - 2026-05-10

### Added
- Initial release of ClawTeam Directors Orchestrator
- 31 world-renowned directors' style profiles
  - 15 Chinese directors (Zhang Yimou, Wong Kar-wai, Ang Lee, etc.)
  - 8 Hollywood directors (Spielberg, Nolan, Tarantino, etc.)
  - 8 European/Asian art house directors (Bergman, Kurosawa, Miyazaki, etc.)
- Three multi-agent collaboration modes:
  - Sequential Chain for creative relay
  - Debate & Voting for option comparison
  - Mixture of Agents (MoA) for chief director coordination
- Individual director skills in `skills/` directory
- Comprehensive README with usage examples
- Director matching script for automated recommendations
- MIT License

### Features
- **Multi-Agent Orchestration**: Coordinate multiple director agents for collaborative creativity
- **Style Analysis**: Deep dive into each director's core philosophy, aesthetic features, and narrative techniques
- **Trigger Word System**: Activate specific director perspectives or collaborative modes
- **Use Case Examples**: Scriptwriting, visual design, narrative optimization, short film creation
- **Cross-Cultural Fusion**: Combine Eastern and Western cinematic perspectives
