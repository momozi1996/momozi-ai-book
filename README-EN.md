```
Compose a short story themed on loss and remembrance
```

**Agent Output (Yu Hua Tone):**
My father passed away when I was cutting wheat in the field. I was seven years old back then, and the ripe wheat stretched golden all over the land. He promised to take me to town for wonton soup once we finished harvesting this field.

I never got that bowl of wonton soup.

He left this world with a gentle smile on his face, just like he was merely tired and wanted a short rest. I called out his name repeatedly but received no response. I shook his body yet there was no reaction. Sharp wheat awns pricked my palms without bringing physical pain, yet tears streamed down my cheeks uncontrollably.

I learned later that he died from extreme exhaustion, having worked three consecutive days nonstop to finish harvesting before the rainy season arrived. Even in his final moment, his hands still tightly clutched a handful of ripe wheat ears.

I kept that bundle of wheat for forty whole years.
